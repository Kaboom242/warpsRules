Village (General)Government
   Most towns are administrated by a Village Elder or Village Council appointed by the people of the town.
   Audience: Typically Village leadership is willing to meet with most visitors as long as their request is reasonable.
   Peacekeeping: Villages will keep the peace themselves, with most having an Old Sword or Spear they can use.
Crime/Punishment
