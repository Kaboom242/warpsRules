Hooks
1 :
Missing Person: Someone has gone missing in the caves and needs to be found, possibly a friend or an important figure.
2 :
Ghost Mining Village: Deserted village and all the mining equipment, looks like everyone disappeared yesterday.
3 :
Carved Obelisk: Standing alone, an obelisk with ancient writing depicts an evil sealed.
4 :
Disappearing Miners: Miners have been disappearing in the deeper tunnels, and no one knows why. Their families seek answers and closure.
5 :
Strange Rumblings: Unusual tremors and rumblings are felt throughout the cave system. Something big and possibly dangerous is moving underground.
6 :
Glowing Mushrooms: A cluster of bioluminescent mushrooms is spreading rapidly, and their glow is attracting all sorts of creatures, both benign and hostile.
7 :
Echoes of the Past: Ancient murals and carvings have been discovered in a newly opened cavern, depicting a lost civilization. What happened to them?
8 :
The Lost City: Rumors speak of a lost underground city filled with treasures and dangers, hidden deep within the cave system.
9 :
The Crystal Cavern: A cavern filled with rare and valuable crystals has been discovered, but it's guarded by territorial creatures.
10 :
Stolen Children: Children have gone missing from nearby villages, and tracks lead into the caves. Who or what is taking them?
11 :
The Cursed Pool: A pool of water deep in the caves is said to be cursed, causing madness and death to those who drink from it.
12 :
The Forgotten Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to a long-forgotten god lies deep within the caves, filled with traps and guardians.
13 :
The Dark Elf Threat: A community of Dark Elfs is plotting an attack on the surface world, and the party must stop them before it's too late.
14 :
The Underground River: An underground river has been discovered, potentially leading to uncharted parts of the cave system, or even to another realm.
15 :
The Guardian Beast: A powerful beast is guarding something precious in the caves. To claim the treasure, the beast must be defeated or appeased.
16 :
The Slaver's Den: A group of slavers is using part of the cave system as a base to imprison and trade captives. Free the slaves and stop the slavers.
17 :
The Cavern of Whispers: A cavern where the very walls seem to whisper secrets and lies, driving those who stay too long to madness.
18 :
The Chasm: A massive chasm splits the cave system, and something is stirring in its depths. Exploring it could be highly dangerous, but potentially rewarding.
19 :
The Illithid Colony: An illithid (mind flayer) colony has been discovered, plotting to expand their influence and enslave more minds.
20 :
The Lava Tubes: Lava tubes deep in the earth are both a hazard and a potential source of power. Navigating them requires skill and caution.
21 :
The Sealed Door: A massive, sealed door has been found deep within the caves. What lies beyond it? And why was it sealed?
