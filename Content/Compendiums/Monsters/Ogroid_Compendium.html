Ogre
  Ogroid
  v2.0
Description
Huge monsterous brutes, they are typically 10' to 12' tall, are covered in muscle and very thick skin. They are strong enough to bend tree trucks, which they often use as clubs, and can shake off incredible amounts of damage. Extremely dangerous, they can easily take on multiple foes at once.

They tend to live a solitary life in caves, typically sleeping for days at a time. They collected the belongings of those they kill, especially prizing shiny objects. Those who enter an Ogre cave will be attacked mercelessly. Ogres prefer to break the bones of those they kill, leaving them alive to be boiled for food. Elves are considered a special delicacy.

Ogres will sometimes join large goblin war bands with a particularly good promise of reasure and loot. These alliances will typically break down eventually, often very much at the goblin's expense.

Hill Giant

  v2.0
Description
About 20' tall. They live in hilly areas and sleep most of the year, sometimes venturing out to eat local people and/or livestock.

They are very stupid and speak and understand very little of the Common tongue. Tend to guard a small collection of Gold (20-30) and Rare Items they have hoarded over the years.

Will join Goblins and other monsters for short term benefits but it rarely lasts due to a tendancy to get hungry and attept to eat their allies.
