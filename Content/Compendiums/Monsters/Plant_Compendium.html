Description
A living tree, Entens are typically calm, wise, and rational. They spend most of their time in isolated communities deep in the forest and most of that time sleeping, rarely making contact with sentient creatures.

Entens are most active when the parts of the forest they call their home are in danger, and will fight ferociously to defend it.

Of the sentient creatures, Forest Elves are those Entens hold in the highest regard, and see them as the most wise of the 'Child' speicies.

Rarely, Entens can catch a desease called the Red Moss, where an Enten loses most of it's intellect and seeks to eat the living, particularly sentient creatures.
