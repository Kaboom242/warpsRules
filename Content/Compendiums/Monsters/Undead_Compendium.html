Description
Reanimaed human corpses that typically feast on the brains and flesh of the living. These creatures are often reanimated by evil magic and can be under the command of a Necromancer.

Have no sentience, no fear, and will continue attacking until called off or destroyed. Unless commanded, Zombies do not attempt tactics and will move towards prey without fear. The Zombies primary attack is a Grapple then Bite, and if possible multiple Zomibes biting a single target.

Drowned
  Zombie
  v2.0
Description
Drowned are zombies that died in the sea, typically having been drowned. While they have been known to eat flesh, they seek most of all to drowned other forms of life for reasons few understand. They are often found around shepwrecks, particualy those in 'cursed waters'.

Sailors say that those who get close to them can hear them muttering about 'absolution of the waves'. Few have survived these encounters however and fewer still have tried to determine what this might mean.
