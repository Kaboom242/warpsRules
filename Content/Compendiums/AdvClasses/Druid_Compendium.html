Basic Pure Multiclass Mini Class
Class Info
Druid Druid are people who have an intense connection to the natural world. They almost always feel more comfortable out in nature then in cities or other crowded places. Druids almost always worship Ernok, the God of Nature, and are devoted to protecting the harmony of nature. Druids can come from all walks of life, but all find themselves plled ot wild, untamed lands where nature's power and beauty is at it's strongest.
Dominions are the parts of the world that the Druid may connect with those places by with them.
Attuning to a Domain: Druid must be a Survivalist 2 in that domain, and fufill the point cost that domain on thier Druidic Aspect Card.
Companions are much like a pet, but with a more powerful and personal connection. Gain a Druid Companion Card.
Communions involve channeling the power of nature through the Druid's body. This can alter the area arond the Druid, but does take a tole on his Body. Wither is the drain this takes on the Druid's life energies.
Brewing allows the Druid to use his knowledge of the natural world to make powerful concotions. These Liquors can have effects ranging from healing, to temporarily transforming the body of the drinker.
Guardians are the creatures of the wilderness that can be summoned to do fight alongside the Druid. A Druid must first Summon the creature, and then attempt to Command it, lest it run away. A Guardians Summon Cost and Command Points are listed alongside it.
Druid Staffs can be gained and reparied within nature and can enhance a Druid's power. Basic

Those that leave society behind to become more in tune with nature may become Druid's, those blessed by Ernok, the God of nature to protect the natural world. Druids are often reclusive figures, living deep within the forest and primarily concerned with protecting these areas from the destruction and corruption of the civilized world. Druids can speak to animals, brew healing draughts, control plants, and even summon the animals of the wild to aid them.

Guardian of The Grove
Druid 3
+
Verdant Domain
The Guardian of the Grove is a person who feels a closer connection to plants than other people and feels their power run through him, even becoming part plant themselves. The Guardian of the Grove is deeply concerned with protecting nature from anything that might threaten it and will go to extreme means for that protection. The Guardian of the Grove can control vines, create thorns and poisonous spores, and even summon trees to life and battle.
Guardian of The Overgrowth
Druid 3
+
Lush Domain
The Guardian of the Overgrowth gains his powers from the deepest and darkest jungles. He is in tune with many plants and insects of these places and immune to their poisons. He can use the exotic and deadly poisons these places produce to choke and debilitate his foes as well as call on the power of the overgrown jungles themselves.
Guardian of The Wastes
Druid 3
+
Arid Domain
The Guardian of the Wastes walks the barren deserts as if they were a home, often wearing bones and reptile skins. They are unbothered by the extreme heat and are skilled at finding water to survive in the harshest environments. A Guardian of the Wastes can use sand to hide and smother his foes, and even become sand to evade attacks.
Guardian of The Winter
Druid 3
+
Arctic Domain
The Guardian of the Winter makes his home in the coldest parts of the world, such as high peaks and tundra. He wears several layers of skins and seems bothered by this deep cold. The Guardian of Winter instead can control the snows and ice of these regions to form deadly icicles and powerful cold winds.
Guardian of The Torent
Druid 3
+
Freshwater Domain
The Guardian of the Torrent is most in tune with the rivers and lakes of the world. They prefer to spend their time meditating under waterfalls and in the many hidden caves of the running water. The Guardian of the Torrent can bend the flow of the water to wash out his enemies, as well as ride jets of water high into the air.
Guardian of The Tides
Druid 3
+
Ocean Domain
The Guardian of the Tides is harmony with creatures of vast and dark ocean depths to the point that they have started to take on their features. The Guardian of the Tides wishes to keep the oceans pure and protected and sees the ocean as a living entity of the utmost power and grace. These Druids can summon ocean tentacles, create waves and waterfalls, and summon swarms of jellyfish and krakens to their aid.

Due to the dangerous nature of their crafts and it's uncanny effects, they are both shunned by society and sought out for their power, an arrangement they are often perfectly happy to use to their advantage. Witches may create acid vials, potions which transform the body of the drinker, and even bring the dead back to life, but always for a price.
